How Was Molecular Docking Done?

Molecular docking was performed on four different DENV3 envelope protein structures
(PDB IDs: 1UZG, 7A3P, 3VTT, 7A3R).

Additionally, docking was conducted with representative structures from DENV1 (3G7T), DENV2 (1OAN), and DENV4 (3WE1) to their respective target proteins.

In this project, AutoDock Vina was the primary tool for performing molecular docking.
